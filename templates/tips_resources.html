Tips & Resources for Net Zero Readiness

About the Survey / सर्वेक्षण के बारे में

This survey is conducted by IIT BHU to help MSMEs achieve the Net Zero Emission (NZE) target set by the Government of India. It enables MSMEs to improve sustainability, efficiency, and long-term profitability.

भारत सरकार द्वारा निर्धारित शून्य शुद्ध उत्सर्जन (NZE) लक्ष्य को प्राप्त करने में एमएसएमई की सहायता हेतु यह सर्वेक्षण IIT BHU द्वारा किया जा रहा है।

Understanding Your Maturity Level

Your maturity level indicates how prepared your MSME is in adopting Net Zero practices, energy efficiency, ESG reporting, and sustainable operations.

  • Basic: Awareness stage, limited data, informal practices
  • Developing: Initial planning, basic energy/resource controls
  • Mature: Structured sustainability programs, monitoring systems
  • Advanced: Industry leader, net-zero aligned strategy & reporting

Practical Tips for MSMEs

  • Start tracking electricity, fuel, and water consumption monthly
  • Replace inefficient motors, lighting, and compressors
  • Conduct periodic energy audits (internal or certified)
  • Train employees on energy-saving behaviors
  • Adopt digital record-keeping for ESG and compliance
  • Explore rooftop solar and waste heat recovery

Government Policies & Schemes (India)

  • National Action Plan on Climate Change (NAPCC)
  • MSME Sustainable (ZED) Certification Scheme
  • Perform, Achieve & Trade (PAT) Scheme
  • Credit Linked Capital Subsidy Scheme (CLCSS)
  • Rooftop Solar Programme – MNRE
  • Green MSME Financing by SIDBI
